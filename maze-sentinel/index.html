How It Works
  • Collect shards to charge your pulse field.
  • Deploy pulse boost to stun and reroute nearby drones.
  • Use gates and corners to break pursuit lines.
  • Survive long enough to trigger Overdrive mode.
Visual / gameplay hooks
  • Dynamic fog, scanlines, bloom-like glows, and particle trails
  • Enemy steering with route bias instead of raw direct chase
  • Mobile touch controls + keyboard support
  • Great base for future bosses, gadgets, and procedural districts
Controls: WASD / arrows to move • Space or Pulse Boost button to discharge • Avoid contact with drones.
